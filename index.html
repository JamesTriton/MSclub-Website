About the Club

Being among the best technical institutes, techies from around the world have seen a suite of events organised here with most of them organised by the Microsoft Campus Club. The club was established in 2005 and has been a trailblazer since then, among all other technical clubs of the institute. From edifying codes to the minimalist designs,it organises quite a handful of events . The club not only focuses on the learning process of newbie coders but also provides them with worthy field days in the form of organisation of various events. The club begins its year with the most awaited pre innovision event of the institute, "Treasherlocked". Each year the club witnesses around thousands of participants from around the globe indulging themselves in extracting solutions for the warped questions from their rapidly firing neurones. Apart from organising events that challenge the brain, the club also organises an open coding competition "Codenigma" on platforms like Hackerrank and Codechef, evaluating codes of immatures and perfectionists on the same platform, a platform which provides knowledge, experience and exposure to each participant. Apart from events, the club keeps training the members around with the learning of various codes of various platforms. The club also involves itself in designing and proffers the ideas of the members of the design team through the events. All of it's events are well articulated and presented by the content team on it's website and other social modules. From dawn to dusk, the club members try to bring the best possible events throughout the year for the students around.
